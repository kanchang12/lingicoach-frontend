AI system disclosure (EU AI Act Article 50)

Lingi Coach is powered by Google Gemini AI. All responses are generated by an artificial intelligence system. You are interacting with an AI, not a human coach. This disclosure is made in compliance with EU AI Act Article 50.

What data we collect

Account data: your email address and name, collected when you register. Stored securely in Supabase (EU-hosted infrastructure).

Usage data: your chosen language, practice streak, and completed scenarios. Stored in your account so your progress is saved across devices.

Payment data: processed entirely by LemonSqueezy. We do not store card details. We receive only confirmation of payment and your email.

Conversation data: your chat sessions are processed in real time by Google Gemini AI and held in server memory only during the active session. They are automatically deleted when the session ends (within 30 minutes). We do not store conversation content.

What we do not collect

We do not use cookies for tracking. We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers. We do not store conversation content after your session ends.

Children

Lingi Coach is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has registered, please contact us immediately. COPPA compliant.

Data retention

Account data is retained until you delete your account. Conversation data is deleted automatically at session end. Payment records are retained as required by law.

Security

All data is transmitted over TLS. Passwords are hashed by Supabase Auth. Database access is protected by row-level security — users can only access their own data. Sessions are isolated by unique ID.
